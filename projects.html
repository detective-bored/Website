Everything I Wish I Said

A public forum project where users are able to write messages to people or places who they can’t normally speak to as form of catharsis. The entire process is anonymous to allow for more openness towards the process. It does not have to be extremely emotional and deep, it can be as simple as wishing spending more time with someone or wishing you told your roommate to do the dishes more often or as complex as a confession of love. ↗
